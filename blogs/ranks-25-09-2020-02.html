Some Historical Ranks in Indian History After Independence

    Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw (1914–2008) on 1 January 1973. Field Marshal Kondandera Madappa Cariappa(1899–1993) on 14 January 1986. Marshal of the Indian Air Force Arjan Singh(1919-2017) on January 2002. Cheif Of Defence Staff Bipin Rawat(1958-till date) on 31 December 2019.
** This ranks are not given to everyone but only to some seeing the circumstances of the countries development, borders issues, International Issues and the quality of service. No doubt all officers are great but these are the officers who have served during hardship.
For Example CDS Bipin Rawat has worked with governement for Indias First Surgical Strike followed by Second.
