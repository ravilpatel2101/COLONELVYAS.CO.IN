About Col Vyas

Colonel Prakash P Vyas is an ever blessed sincere Farmer, dedicated Soldier(commando), Highly professional Helicopter Commercial Pilot, Inspirational speaker, Mind Power trainer, Psychotherapist, Pranayam-Yog Instructor, Free Mason, Vipashya Meditator, Grapho therapist in making, from Ranpar village of Amreli District. He is presently residing in Rajkot, who has decided to dedicate his next 20 years of life for the society, by way of Teaching Yog-Pranayam and practising Psychotherapy.

Aspiring Personality

He provides guidance to all High aspirant students, including the one desirous of joining defence forces, civil services and in various fields, through out the day till 11 pm everyday. He is Conducting Mind power Seminars, Motivational lectures, personal Councelling without charging anything to any individual and various educational institutes and corporates too.

Infact he has started three COVID-19 Hospitals which are running on no Profit no Loss basis will provide treatment completly free of cost soon! He has also started with Rural Cardiac Care Digital Program in order to help people in Rural areas also with the facilities of Urban Areas.
