PSYCHOTHERAPY

  • WHAT IS PSYCHOTHERAPY?

    • Psychotherapy refers to a range of treatment that can help with mental health problems emotional challenges and some psychiatric disorders.It is a collaborative treatment based on the relationship between individual and a psychologist. It provides a supportive environment that allows you to talk openly with someone who is objective neutral and non judgemental.

  • HOW PSYCHOTHERAPY WORKS?

    • You and your psychologist will work together to identify and change the thought and behaviour pattern that are keeping you from feeling your best.In psychotherapy, psychologist apply scientifically validated procedure to help people develop healthy hair more effective habits.Psychotherapy can provide help with a range of problems, from depression and low self-esteem to addiction and family disputes. Anyone who is feeling overwhelmed by their problems and unable to cope may be able to benefit from psychotherapy.
